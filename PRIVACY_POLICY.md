# Privacy Policy for Shruti

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-16

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Shruti** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
**We do not collect any personal data.**
Shruti is an offline-first utility application designed for ear training and pitch recognition. We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

### 2.1 Permissions
Our application requires no special sensitive permissions (such as Microphone, Camera, or Location) to function.
- **Microphone**: Not used.
- **Internet**: Not used for data collection.

## 3. Data Storage
All progress, scores, and history are stored **locally on your device**.
- We do not transmit this data to any external servers.
- If you uninstall the application or clear its data, your progress will be lost.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that track user behavior.
- **Google Play Services**: The app is distributed via Google Play, which may collect anonymous diagnostic data (like crash logs) subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Since we do not collect any personal information, our application is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
